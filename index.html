What business owners typically discover
COMMON FINDINGS
Finding #1 — The phone number problem
On mobile, the phone number isn't tappable — or it's buried three scrolls down. Customers on their phone can't call you in one tap. That's calls lost daily.
Finding #2 — Too many choices
Six navigation links. Three different buttons. No clear direction. When customers don't know what to do next, they do nothing. They leave.
Finding #3 — No trust signals above the fold
Reviews, licenses, years in business — these build trust fast. Most sites bury them. First 5 seconds matter more than the rest of the page combined.
Finding #4 — The site loads slow on mobile
A site that takes 4+ seconds to load loses more than half its mobile visitors before they even see it. Speed is trust.
